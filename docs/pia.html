Philippine International Aid (PIA) is a nonprofit organization is now on its 32nd year of providing educational assistance to indigent children in Manila, Philippines. Currently thousands children attend school through the efforts of PIA and partner agencies in Manila. And hundreds of children have turned into productive adults, in turn alleviating poverty for their families.

In middle school, I've presented to hundreds of sponsors in the Hyatt Grand Ballroom based in San Francisco where I gave a speech on providing for the children in the Philippines. I've managed to raise thousands of dollars.

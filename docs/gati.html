Gifted and Talented Innovators (GATI) is a program that helps students prepare for college and beyond by taking immediate action and starting real companies. The mentors there coach students to work in teams, employ 21st century skills, and adopt Silicon Valley's innovative state of mind so that they can go from an idea to execution, to growth, and to global impact.

In this program, I had the opportunity to meet mentors and CEOs that teach me entrepreneurship skills that will help me in my future career. I also visited large companies to see how they worked on the inside such as Udacity, Salesforce, and Google. My team and I started a startup company, pitched to Silicon Valley investors such as Y-combinator, and executed the company successfully.
